About

Hi, I’m Juan Fernando Pinzon Moreno, an aspiring Tech Consultant and Junior Software Developer with a diverse background in film production, digital marketing, and full-stack development. Over the course of my career, I’ve had the privilege of collaborating with multinational companies such as KPMG, Facebook, British Gas, Telemundo, Qualcomm, UNICEF, and Señal Colombia, where I worked primarily as a content producer. This experience allowed me to gain invaluable skills in project management across diverse environments and industries. ... Driven by a passion for technology and problem-solving, I completed a Full Stack Developer bootcamp at the Code Institute, specializing in building web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Django. As someone who thrives in collaborative environments, I’m excited to merge my technical skills with the project management experience I developed working with global brands. My decision to transition into the tech industry came from a desire to be part of the evolution that is reshaping the world. I believe technology is driving meaningful change, and I want to contribute to this dynamic, ever-evolving field. I’m particularly motivated by the opportunity to create tech solutions that not only help businesses thrive but also address the needs of people who require support and innovative solutions. Let’s build something amazing together!
